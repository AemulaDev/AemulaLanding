Aemula

A truly decentralized, DAO-moderated, subscription journalism platform incentivizing the sharing of unbiased, diverse, and high-quality information.

"stimulos dedit aemula virtus"

rivalry in excellence spurs (them) on

  1. read articles - machine learning algorithm orders feed with intent of promoting diverse and moderate viewpoints
  2. write articles - get paid for each person in agreement
  3. moderate articles - join the DAO and earn rewards for moderating content
  4. truly decentralized - no centralized control of content flow or data storage
